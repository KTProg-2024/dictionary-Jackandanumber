SINCE 1828

client

noun

cli·​ent | \ ˈklī-ənt

Definition of client

  1. : a person who engages the professional advice or services of another
    2. //a lawyer's clients
  2. : customer
    3. //a restaurant's clients
  3. : a person served by or utilizing the services of a social agency
  4. : a computer in a network that uses the services (such as access to files or shared peripherals) provided by a server

Examples of client in a Sentence

//The accountant is meeting with another client right now, but she'll be able to see you later this afternoon.

//a law firm soliciting new clients through television advertising
